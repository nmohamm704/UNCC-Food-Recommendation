FoodMatch
What is FoodMatch?
FoodMatch is a platform that helps users discover restaurants based on their preferences and location.
How do I search for restaurants?
You can use the search bar at the top of the page to look for restaurants by name, cuisine type, or location.
Is FoodMatch free to use?
Yes, FoodMatch is completely free to use for finding and exploring restaurants.
Can I leave reviews for restaurants?
Currently, FoodMatch does not support user reviews, but we are working on adding this feature soon.
